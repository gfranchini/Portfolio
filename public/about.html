About Me

I am a recent college graduate from the University of Florida in search of a fun and rewarding career. Although I graduated with a degree in Biology, my affinity toward the tech culture has led me to join the Wyncode Academy. It is a privilege to work among others who are just as passionate about tech as I am.

Throughout my life I have always had a deep curiosity toward computers and how they worked. Like many before me, video games have sparked my interest in programming. Where does the logic come from? How can code create digital worlds?

After spending many hours manipulating lines of code to perform certain tasks while metaphorically pulling out my hair, the end result always tends to exceed the pain and struggle. Although there are many times I feel like quitting, my curiosity will always linger, and that will always push me to go forward no matter the difficulty.
